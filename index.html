FAQ

Is it a study or social event?

Both! TMG is a place where you can study, socialize, or grab a snack and relax. We occasionally have optional activities for guests to participate in if they wish.

What time and where are the events?

We currently hold events every Friday from 7PM to 11PM at California Hall (unless announced otherwise). Make sure to follow our Instagram to stay updated!

Is the RSVP mandatory?

Yes, we are now requiring all guests to RSVP by the deadline in order to attend our events (no exceptions). Please make sure you stay updated via our Instagram and sign up on time!

What payment methods do you take?

We currently accept Venmo, Zelle, cash, and tap payments via Square.

ABOUT

The Midnight Grind (TMG) is a student-run, late-night cafe located on-campus at UC Davis, created to meet students' need for a safe and welcoming space after dark to study and socialize. Our team of over 50 dedicated volunteers-executive board, officers, and host members-work together to provide one of the few spots for healthy nightlife in a town where nearly everything closes by 8PM.

MISSION

At TMG, we support students academically and holistically to do their best work and create a positive impact on the world. We are motivated by our belief that each of our guests have a unique ability to do good in the world, and we believe in championing them to live our their lives healthily and purposefully-no matter what time it is.
